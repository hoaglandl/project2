About Me

I grew up in Marlton, New Jersey, but I am currently living in Orlando. I graduated in 2017 with a BA in English Writing from Rider University in Lawrenceville, NJ. I moved to Orlando in late 2017 to work at Walt Disney World as a part of their Disney College Program internship. I permanently moved to Orlando after the internship and began working for Disney full-time. Now, I am excited about pursuing a Master's Degree in Communications and Web Design from the University of Florida.


My favorite things to do are outdoor activities like hiking, going to the beach, and swimming in the pool. I also enjoy artsy activities like painting and drawing. I enjoy web designing for its creative outlet that is also practical and compliments my writing degree.
